I wouldn't mind switching to artificial meat if it met the following criteria:

1. Higher weight-to-protein ratio _than meat_.
2. Cheaper than _meat_.
3. Has all the other (non-protein) benefits of _meat_.
4. Has no side-effects.
5. Similar taste and texture.
